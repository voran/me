Bio

I was born in Sofia, Bulgaria on 15 September 1989. This makes me exactly old. I was born into a family of scientists, and was raised to love science in all its forms. I also developed an early interest in music, and started playing the piano at the age of 6. Nowadays I mostly play guitar and clarinet. I also enjoy travelling and outdoor photography. I love hiking, camping, skiing, and swimming.

Education

I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science and Economics with a minor in Math from the American University in Bulgaria. I also spent one semester in the Otto-von-Guericke University in Magdeburg, Germany as an Erasmus Student. During my senior year, I received the Bulgarian Young Leaders Award for 2011 and spent three quarters at the Rochester Institute of Technology.

Interests

My interests in Computer Science are in the areas of Operating Systems, Algorithms, and Distributed Systems. I think P != NP, and I'll live to see it proven. I like Java, but I also like the low-level stuff: C, Assembly. I run Arch Linux on all my devices, except my phone. It runs MeeGo.

My interests in Economics are mainly in the field of Economic Policy. I believe that the economy needs some regulation, because competition is *not* perfect in the real world. There is no perfect information. Most markets suffer from economies of scale.
